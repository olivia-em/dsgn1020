The Devil is your shadow self and all the things that are holding you back from being the happiest you can be. Stop choosing the path of least resistance because it feels good now or you feel like you don’t have any control. You have the control. Be self-aware.
The Hanged Man encourages you to pause. Stop moving without going anywhere. Reflect. Develop a new perspective. You won’t make any changes by continuing on the same path, so take a second to decide on a new direction. If you’re struggling with yourself, brute force or ignoring the problem will do nothing. Intellectualize what’s going on.
Temperance is here to remind you to be patient and search for balance. If life is feeling stressful or overwhelming, pause and remain calm. Everything takes time, but before you even realize, life will be different. Be kind to yourself. Again, brute force will not create stable, longterm change.
